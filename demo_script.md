# 🎬 2-Minute Demo Video Script

Today, thousands of food and beverage wholesalers are running their entire operation through WhatsApp, Email, Phone, or in-person. 

Orders come in as voice notes, images, short messages — often unclear, incomplete, or just "the usual."

The result? Manual overhead, costly errors, and fulfilment that doesn't scale.

Kuna changes that.

We plug directly into the channels your customers already use — WhatsApp, Email, Phone — and our AI agent interprets every inbound message as an order in real time. Text, recurring orders, even a photo of a handwritten list — Kuna extracts the order, validates it against your SKU catalog, and proactively clarifies anything ambiguous before it becomes a problem.

Instead of chaos, you get structured, actionable orders — zero friction.

Orders flow seamlessly from Pending → Confirmed → Fulfilled, with automated customer comms at every stage. If something's ambiguous — like "the usual" — Kuna confirms interpretation before locking it in. That's a massive reduction in order error rate, out of the box.

Inside the dashboard, your ops team gets full visibility: an order queue, flagged items that need attention, and aggregate demand data for smarter inventory planning.

You stay in control — override anything, send AI-suggested replies, or let the automation handle routine confirmations end-to-end.

And here's the real unlock:

We require zero behaviour change. Chefs keep ordering the way they always have. Wholesalers get a single pane of glass. We meet your customers where they are and convert messy, unstructured conversations into clean, reliable commerce.

Kuna: from messaging chaos to operational clarity — powered by AI agents.
