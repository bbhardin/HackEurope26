# 🎯 30-Second Pitch (Core Message)

Food & beverage wholesalers across Europe receive most of their orders via disaggregated communication channels such as WhatsApp — these are messy, unstructured, and manual.
Kuna uses AI agents to turn those messages into structured, confirmed orders — with built-in clarification, fulfilment workflows, and ERP-ready data.

We reduce errors, save hours of back-office work every day, and give wholesalers a clean, real-time overview of customers, orders, and stock — all without changing how their customers order.

Kuna turns whatever communication channel wholesalers' customers use into an intelligent order management system.

🎬 2-Minute Demo Video Script

Today, thousands of food and beverage wholesalers manage their business through WhatsApp, Email, Phone, or in-Person.
Orders come in as voice notes, images, short messages — often unclear, incomplete, or referencing “the usual.”

This creates manual work, errors, and costly fulfilment mistakes.

Kuna changes that.

Kuna connects directly to your WhatsApp, Email, or Phone and automatically understands customer messages as incoming orders.
Whether it’s text, recurring orders, or even a photo of a handwritten list — Kuna extracts the order, checks it against your SKU catalog, and clarifies anything unclear.

Instead of chaos, you get structured orders.

Orders move cleanly from Pending to Confirmed to Fulfilled, with separate customer communication at each step.
If something is ambiguous — like “the usual” — Kuna confirms interpretation before locking it in. This dramatically reduces order errors.

Inside the dashboard, you see:
	•	A clear order queue
	•	Flagged items that need attention
	•	Customer health insights
	•	Aggregate product demand for stock planning

You can manually override anything, send smart suggested replies, or let automation handle routine confirmations.

And the best part?

We don't ask anyone to change how they work. The chefs keep on using Whatsapp, Email, or Phone. The wholesaler gets a dashboard.

Kuna meets the customers where they are without requiring behaviour change — turning conversations into clean, reliable commerce.

Kuna: from messaging chaos to operational clarity — powered by AI agents.
